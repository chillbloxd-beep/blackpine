Responsible Disclosure

Report a security concern responsibly.

This page explains how to report security issues affecting Blackpine Cybersecurity Inc.'s own website. It is not permission to perform unauthorized testing.

How to contact Blackpine

Email security@blackpinecybersecurity.com with a concise description of the issue, the affected URL, browser or device context if relevant, and clear reproduction details that do not cause harm. This security mailbox is a configurable placeholder for responsible reports.

Responsible reporting expectations

Please do not access, modify, delete, disrupt, or exfiltrate data. Do not test third-party systems, customer environments, vendors, or infrastructure that is not owned or explicitly authorized by Blackpine Cybersecurity Inc.

Provide enough detail for review without including passwords, secret keys, regulated data, or unnecessary personal information. Screenshots or short descriptions are preferred over intrusive testing.

Review process

Blackpine will review responsible reports when possible. Submission does not create a client relationship, employment relationship, bounty obligation, or authorization to continue testing.
