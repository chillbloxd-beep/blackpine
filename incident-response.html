Response Process

A measured process for urgent security concerns.

Every situation is different. Blackpine uses a structured approach to help your team move from uncertainty to informed action.

01

Intake

Collect the initial facts, contacts, affected systems, and business priorities.

02

Triage

Assess likely severity, immediate risks, and information needed for next steps.

03

Containment

Guide steps to limit access, preserve accounts, and reduce ongoing exposure.

04

Investigation support

Help review available evidence and coordinate with providers or internal teams.

05

Recovery guidance

Support safe restoration of accounts, services, and normal business workflows.

06

Hardening recommendations

Document practical controls to reduce repeat incidents and improve readiness.
