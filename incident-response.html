What To Do First

Preserve context and reduce avoidable damage.

These first steps help keep the situation understandable while you decide who needs to be involved. They are general guidance, not a substitute for legal, insurance, or emergency advice.

  1. 01

    Preserve relevant emails and logs.

  2. 02

    Avoid deleting suspicious account activity.

  3. 03

    Secure critical accounts where possible.

  4. 04

    Record a timeline of suspicious events.

  5. 05

    Avoid sharing sensitive details through insecure channels.
