Incident Response

Steady support when something feels wrong.

Blackpine helps organizations triage suspicious account activity, phishing incidents, business email compromise, ransomware concerns, and potential data exposure.

Request response supportPrepare in advance

Response support

We help contain issues, preserve key information, coordinate next steps, and guide communications with technology providers, insurers, and legal counsel where appropriate.

Readiness planning

We build practical playbooks, escalation paths, account recovery procedures, evidence checklists, and tabletop exercises for small and mid-sized teams.

Common scenarios

Compromised inboxes, suspicious MFA prompts, payroll or payment diversion, unauthorized cloud access, malicious attachments, and exposed credentials.

After-action improvement

After urgent work is complete, we identify root causes and recommend sustainable controls to reduce recurrence.
