Cybersecurity Checklist for Growing Businesses

A practical checklist page for reviewing accounts, access, recovery, and response readiness. No PDF download is implemented on this static site.

Checklist

  • Require MFA for email, cloud, finance, and administrator accounts.
  • Review administrator permissions and remove unused accounts.
  • Confirm backups exist and can be restored.
  • Document who handles suspicious emails, account lockouts, and website issues.
  • Verify staff onboarding and offboarding steps for key systems.
  • Review vendor access to files, inboxes, websites, and business systems.
  • Maintain a short incident contact list for leadership and technology providers.
