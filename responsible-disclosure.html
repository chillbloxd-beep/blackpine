Responsible Disclosure

Report a security concern responsibly.

If you believe you have found a security issue related to Blackpine Cybersecurity Inc., please report it carefully and avoid accessing, changing, or exposing data.

How to report

Email contact@blackpinecyber.com with a concise description, affected URL or system, and safe reproduction context. Do not include sensitive data, passwords, or personal information.

Expectations

Please act in good faith, avoid disruption, and do not perform testing that could harm systems or data. Blackpine will review legitimate reports when appropriate.
