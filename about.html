About

Practical cybersecurity advice, handled carefully.

Blackpine Cybersecurity Inc. is a Canada-based cybersecurity consultancy for organizations that need clear assessment work, confidential intake, and business-focused reporting.

What Blackpine does

Focused review work for accounts, cloud access, email risk, and incident readiness.

Blackpine helps business owners and operators understand practical cybersecurity risk without turning the process into a jargon-heavy exercise. Work may include security assessments, phishing and email protection reviews, cloud account security, incident response support, and digital risk advisory.

Scope is confirmed before assessment work begins. Initial inquiries should avoid passwords, private keys, regulated records, or unnecessary sensitive evidence.

How Blackpine works

Calm discovery, clear scope, practical output.

The work starts with understanding the business context: what systems matter, who manages them, what has changed, and what decisions need to be made. From there, Blackpine defines scope, reviews the agreed areas, and turns findings into plain-language recommendations.

✓ Confidential intake before detailed review

✓ Written authorization before assessment activity

✓ Evidence-based observations without exploit instructions

✓ Reporting for owners, operators, and technical teams

Who Blackpine helps

Built for organizations that need careful guidance, not a security theatre exercise.

Blackpine is suited to small businesses, startups, law firms, clinics, real estate firms, e-commerce teams, and professional service organizations that rely on email, cloud accounts, websites, and shared business systems.

Engagement principles

Be specific about scope, communicate in plain language, preserve confidentiality, avoid unnecessary access, and focus on improvements a business can actually implement.

What Blackpine does not claim

No cybersecurity service can guarantee complete prevention of incidents. Blackpine does not claim instant recovery, public-sector endorsement, unsupported certifications, or round-the-clock SOC coverage.

Responsible Cybersecurity Only

Authorized, scoped, defensive work.

Blackpine supports authorized, scoped, defensive cybersecurity work only. Blackpine does not support unauthorized access, credential theft, malware deployment, illegal surveillance, data extraction outside agreed scope, bypassing systems without written authorization, or attacks on third-party systems.
